## Full description (max 4000 chars)

AutoStock puts your personal watchlist front and center — on your phone and right inside your car's dashboard via Android Auto.

**Your watchlist, your way**
Add any stock symbol with a single tap and build the list that matters to you. Prices refresh automatically so you always see live market data without lifting a finger.

**Premarket & after-hours coverage**
Markets don't stop at 4pm and neither does AutoStock. During extended trading sessions the app shows the latest premarket or after-hours price alongside the day's regular close, so you always know where things stand before the bell rings.

**Sort however makes sense**
Three sort modes let you cut through the noise instantly:
• Default — keeps your symbols in the order you added them
• A–Z — alphabetical by ticker for quick scanning
• Top Movers — biggest % swings float to the top so you catch the action fast

Your sort preference is remembered across launches and synced to the Android Auto display.

**Glanceable in the car**
The Android Auto integration shows your full watchlist on any compatible head unit with a clean, driver-safe layout. Green and red arrows signal direction at a glance, and the display title tells you whether you're looking at regular, premarket, or after-hours data.

**Light on your phone**
AutoStock is deliberately minimal. No accounts, no ads, no background syncing when you're not using it. Just open it, see your stocks, close it.

**How to use**
1. Tap ＋ to add a stock symbol (AAPL, TSLA, MSFT, etc.)
2. Swipe left on any card to delete it
3. Tap the sort button to cycle through sort modes
4. Connect your phone to a compatible car head unit to see your watchlist in Android Auto

## Notes for submission

- **Privacy policy**: Google Play requires one. Minimum needed: a statement that the app collects no personal data and makes network requests only to Yahoo Finance to fetch publicly available quote data.
- **Android Auto approval**: Apps using the Car App Library go through an additional review queue. Make sure `automotive_app_desc.xml` declares the correct category (`parking` or `navigation` is not correct for this type — check that it is set to a supported template-based app category).
- **Data safety section**: Declare "No data collected" — the app sends no user data to any server.
