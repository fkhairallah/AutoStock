# AutoStock Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 29, 2026**

## Overview

AutoStock is a stock watchlist app for Android and Android Auto. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what data the app does and does not collect.

## Data Collection

AutoStock does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

- No account or sign-in is required
- No analytics or crash reporting SDKs are included
- No advertising SDKs are included
- No location data is accessed
- Your watchlist (stock symbols) is stored **only on your device** using Android's local SharedPreferences storage and is never transmitted anywhere

## Network Requests

The app makes outbound network requests solely to **Yahoo Finance** (`query2.finance.yahoo.com`) to fetch publicly available stock quote data. These requests contain only the stock ticker symbol being queried — no user identity, device identifier, or personal information of any kind is included.

## Third-Party Services

The only external service the app communicates with is Yahoo Finance, whose data is publicly accessible. AutoStock has no affiliation with Yahoo Finance.

## Children's Privacy

AutoStock does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
